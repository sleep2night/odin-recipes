Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are a classic food that is made by boiling potatoes until soft,

then mashing them into a creamy, smooth, or slightly chunky consistency.

They are typically seasoned with salt and often enriched with ingredients

like butter, milk, or cream to enhance their flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds baking potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic peeled
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Boil a large pot of salted water.
  3. Add potatoes and garlic, lower heat to medium, and simmer until potatoes are tender. (15-20 min)
  4. When potatoes are finished, heat milk and butter in a small saucepan over low heat until butter is melted.
  5. Drain potatoes and return to pot. Slowly add warm milk texture and blend it in with a masher/mixer until potatoes are smooth and creamy.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
