Jalapeño Popper Cups

Description

Jalapeño popper cups are bite-sized appetizers made by filling a crispy wonton

or pastry shell with a creamy mixture of cream cheese, cheddar, and diced jalapeños.

Often topped with bacon bits and baked until golden, they offer a perfect balance of spicy,

cheesy, and savory flavors in a portable, crowd-pleasing form.

Ingredients

  • 12 mini phyllo tart shells
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
  • bacon bits

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place phyllo cups onto a baking sheet.
  3. Stir together cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, jalapenos, and hot sauce in a bowl.
  4. Spoon mixture into phyllo cups.
  5. Sprinkle bacon bits on top.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes. Serve warm.
